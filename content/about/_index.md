**FastenerInsight** is an independent technical reference covering fastener standards, coatings, materials, engineering guides, and procurement specifications. It launched in June 2026.

The content here is written for engineers, procurement professionals, quality inspectors, and anyone working with fasteners who needs clear, practical technical guidance — not marketing fluff.

### What you'll find here

- **Standards comparisons** — DIN vs ISO, ASME vs ASTM, legacy specs vs modern equivalents
- **Coating guides** — performance comparisons, compliance requirements, RFQ templates
- **Engineering reference** — material properties, thread specifications, tolerance guidance
- **Procurement notes** — what to specify, what to watch out for, how to avoid common pitfalls
